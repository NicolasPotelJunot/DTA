Lubricant Oil Analysis

These types of analysis have been done since the beginning of the industrial age, to evaluate machine’s performance. Mainly, it consisted in a simple evaluation of its color and clarity or transparency, using the sense of smell, sight and touch.

Nowdays, we use technologies and high complexity instruments to make a series of essays to evaluate the state of the lubricant and most of the components and pieces of a machine.

Once we obtain the results, we process them and we determine not only the state of the oil, but also if it complies with the main functions for which it was originally formulated, providing the client with valuable information and increase the reliability of his machine.

How does our service works?

  • Sampling: The sampling procedure is very important, it must be representative of the state of the machine.
  • Sample Shipping: The samples must reach the lab as soon as possible after their extraction. The samples are identified not only by the package but also by a sheet with their name, machine number, date and hour, and time of use or mileage of the machine, etc.
  • Essays: They are made according to the type of lubricant and its function.
  • Results: They are sent to the technical office of our company, to elaborate a report with the use recommendations and application for the correct maintenance of the equip analysed.

Once finished The report is printed and sent to the user, together with the results.

Some of the essay performed in our laboratory are listed below:

  1. Density
  2. Color
  3. Kinematic Viscosity at 40°C (104°F)
  4. Kinematic Viscosity at 100°C (212°F)
  5. Viscosity index
  6. Inflammation point
  7. Particle analysis

Transformer Dielectric Oil Treatment

The periodic analysis of the transformer’s oils allows us to evaluate its aging process, achieving the preventive and predictive maintenance of the transformer, as these oils are indicators of the transformer’s state and let us distinguish the cause a possible or an effective malfunction.

The transformer is a machine that barely needs maintenance. Among its components, the dielectric oil is the one that requires it the most.

This oil has many functions in electric transformers, such as isolation between components, refrigerations, internal temperature homogenization, among others.

A transformer life expectancy fundamentally depends on how the oil achieves its purpose. Experiences around the world shows that an 85% of transformer failures that render the unit out of service are related to problems in the insulators.

The dielectric ages and experiment both physical and chemical changes that manifest as a degradation of its properties. This aging is catalyzed by high temperatures, rich oxygen environments and metal (e.g Iron and Copper) in the electrical actives parts.

Periodic analysis of the oil allows evaluating the progress of the aging process, fulfilling the preventive and predictive function on the state of the transformer since they serve as a diagnostic element between possible or effective failure.

Experience gained through the years that although the life expectancy of an dielectric oil can not be infinite, it has been statistically established that the life of a transformer can be extended by 40% through the correct use treatment of the dielectric oil.

Our service consists of:

  • Sampling of the transformer dielectric oil
  • Measurement of the characteristic parameters of the sample
  • Make a database entry to the equipment’s history
  • Oil regeneration

Thermography is a technique of temperature measurement through the infrared emission of the equipments. This allows failure detection in production lines, machines, electrical installations, moulds, ovens, furnaces,exhaust pipes, mechanical elements, electronic circuits, etc. Through this technique we can accurately find the origin of a failure before it happens.

This technique not only reduces the restock expenses but also improves the quality of service, reduces improductive times and overtime,increases productivity and offers information about the current state of the installation.

The technology used to provide this service a thermal photography of the elements of interest, very similar to conventional photography, but detecting infrared (thermal radiation) instead of visible radiation. The result is a digital image and a color mapping that represents the temperature in each pixel taken in the photography. This procedure let us notice most malfunctions without the need of putting the equipment out of service or disassemble it, and it can be used with most electrical and mechanical installation like: transformers, electrical panels, transmission lines, electrical motors and generators, ovens and furnaces, internal combustion engines, refrigeration equipments, fluid pipes and practically to every other element where superficial temperature can provide information regarding its internal state.

Every installation stops working for a technical failures, this represents not only inconveniences, but also unforeseen expenses; the adequate thermal diagnosis applied at regular intervals is a preventive maintenance with plenty of advantages.

Used oil based lubricants Treatment

With this service, our company has the objective of treat oil based lubricants that have lost their properties and prevent the generation of industrial waste. We have developed the necessary equipment to increase and improve the lifespan of oil based lubricants used in the industry, trying to reuse resources, save energy and protect the environment.

Oil based lubricants are contaminated during their utilization, because of the interaction with organic and inorganic byproducts of the oxidation, produced by the wear of materials, reducing their quality. When the amount of contaminants is excessive, the lubricant does not longer fulfills its purpose and must be treated in order to avoid its replacement with a new lubricant.

Oil based lubricants are considered potentially dangerous for the environment because of their low biodegradability and the fact that the can spread in large areas of soil or masses of water, making a thin film that prevents the oxygen flow, and producing a significant degradation of the environment. The pollution with hazardous heavy metals also represents both environmental and health risks.

The pouring of oils in the ground, besides contaminating the soil, can also filtrate and contaminate underground aquifers or it can be dragged by rain and contaminate water courses.

Furthermore, used lubricants and oils are commercialized as a cheap alternative fuel, because it has high calorific power. The main environmental issue is the bad management of the oil originated in improper combustions.

Most of these practices ignore their environmental impact and the fact that technical procedures for oil regeneration even exists.

Our industry provides these service to treat at condition these oils and lubricants to be reused properly by different industries.

Our project depends almost exclusively of raising awareness of in different business and industries, and their waste generation.

There are two ways to perform this treatment: taking our mobile treatment plant to the client’s facilities or carrying the client’s wastes to our permanent treatment plant.
