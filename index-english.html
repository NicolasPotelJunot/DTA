Desarrollos Tecnológicos Avanzados

D.T.A. was created in 2003 with the purpose of researching new technologies in the field of renewable energy, and also offering our services in the field of lubrication ,with emphasis in predictive and preventive industrial maintenance. In association with the National Commission for Space Activities (CONAE, for its acronym in spanish), we are part of the Reconvertion plan of Falda del Carmen to research and produce biofuels.

Our company is in Córdoba, Argentina. We have strategically located our administrative office, and both of our laboratories (research and control of product and processes) in the surroundings of the city, right in the encirclement to ease the logistics.

MISSION

We are COMPROMISED to improve the quality of life in our community, inspiring future generations with our job.

VISION

We want to DEVELOP goods and services, and aim to keep innovating on a daily basis.

In DTA we offer a series of goods and services in assistance for a variety of industries such as farming, food, plastic and metal. We already have significantly, consistently and reliably reduced costs for these industries by decreasing the number of unexpected maintenances, not only financial costs, but also we have saved supplies, hours of labor and ambiental gestion.
